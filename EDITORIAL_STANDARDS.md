# RobertBering.com Editorial Standards

1. Every statement must be objectively true.
2. Never overstate an accomplishment.
3. Attribute evaluations to the evaluator.
4. Use baseball terminology the way coaches do.
5. Every feature must either reduce friction or increase credibility.
6. Team participation and individual selection must be described accurately.
7. When in doubt, choose the simpler and more factual wording.
