Why Robert

Projectable left-hander with a proven record of competing and helping teams win.

Robert Bering is a 2027 left-handed pitcher with an athletic 6'4" frame, a three-pitch arsenal featuring both four-seam and two-seam fastballs, proven varsity production, and the character college coaches value in a clubhouse. He combines pitchability, competitiveness, and a team-first mindset with strong academics and a commitment to continuous development.

Pitch Profile

Three-pitch arsenal. Multiple fastball shapes.

Robert features both a four-seam and two-seam fastball, giving him multiple fastball shapes to complement his slider and changeup. The pitch profile is built around attacking the zone, disrupting timing, and continuing to develop as his frame adds strength.

Meet Robert

Every pitch is a challenge.

Robert loves pitching because every pitch requires focus, adjustment, and competition. He enjoys thinking through each at-bat, attacking hitters with a plan, and helping put his team in the best position to win.

He wants to be remembered as a teammate who is dependable, works hard every day, supports everyone in the dugout, and puts the team first. Off the field, Robert is interested in studying business and enjoys coaching younger players because baseball has taught him lessons he wants to pass on.
